Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Introduction

Body Count ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Body Count is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect and store the following information locally on your device only:

  • Intimate encounter records (dates, details, notes)
  • Partner information (names, demographics, ratings)
  • STI test reminders and results
  • Achievement and milestone data
  • Calendar and statistical data

How We Use Your Information

All data collected by Body Count is used exclusively to:

  • Display your personal statistics and history
  • Track achievements and milestones
  • Provide STI test reminders
  • Generate charts and analytics
  • Enable data import/export functionality

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not:

  • Store your data on external servers
  • Transmit your data over the internet
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use cloud backup services (unless you enable iCloud/device backup)
  • Track your usage or collect analytics

Biometric Authentication

If you enable Face ID/Touch ID authentication, your biometric data is:

  • Processed entirely by your device's operating system
  • Never accessed or stored by our app
  • Used only to verify your identity before accessing the app

Data Export and Import

You have full control over your data:

  • Export your data to CSV or JSON format at any time
  • Import existing data from spreadsheets
  • Delete all data permanently from your device

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Manually delete it using the "Clear All Data" feature
  • Uninstall the application

Third-Party Services

Body Count does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

Body Count is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • View all your data within the app
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your data permanently
  • No need to request data deletion from us

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: privacy@bodycount.app

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Optional biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID)
  • Local SQLite database with encryption support
  • No network transmission of sensitive data

Compliance

This app is designed to comply with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Since all data is stored locally and we do not collect or transmit personal information, most data protection regulations do not apply to our processing activities.
